How to Keep Kids Alive During Quarantine

  1. Keep them Fed!

    2. The most important thing to remember during quarantine is food! Kids require 3 meals a day and at least 10 snacks a day. This hobbits. To saitisfy this insatiable appetite, you can use a variety of sources:

    • Instacart: get your groceries delivered directly to you!

      This is the perfect way to have tons of pre-packaged snacks on hand! Don't forget to include some fruits and vegetables!

    • DoorDash: get pre-made food fast!

      This service will deliver hot (or cold) prepared food directly to your door! No need to put on a bra to have your Chick-Fil-A milkshake. Dial it in!

    • Pizza!

      If all else fails, don't forget that pizza delivery is fast and easy. Just grab your favorite pizza app.

    4. Not for you. For them. Have them run around the house. Literally. Like Amelia Bedelia. Go outside. Run circles around the house. Until I say stop.

    6. Grandma. Drop and dash. Open the doors, throw the bags, drive away as quickly as possible. Maybe lose your phone.
